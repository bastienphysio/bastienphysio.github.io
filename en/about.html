Who is Marc Bastien?

Marc Bastien moved to Ottawa from a small, francophone community near Sudbury, Ontario. He studied Human Kinetics and Physiotherapy at the University of Ottawa, where he gained valuable experience working with high-level athletes as well as many patients from neurological, cardiac, and orthopaedic rehab settings. Passionate about a healthy lifestyle and spending time outdoors, Eastern Ontario was a natural fit for Marc. In his spare time, he enjoys playing hockey with friends and snowshoeing, hiking, or fishing with his family.

Continuing Education

Since graduating from the University of Ottawa, Marc has completed courses in Lumbar Mechanical Diagnosis and Treatment (Robin Mckenzie Institute of Canada), acupuncture and Dry Needling (APTEI), as well as his Level 1 Manual Therapy certification from the Canadian Physiotherapy Association. Marc has also completed over 150 hours of training in Global Postural Reeducation (RPG), a specific manual therapy approach to treat various musculoskeletal conditions as well as postural syndromes like scoliosis.
